Dutchess Bridle & Saddle, LLC

Visual design, Branding, Social Media and Email Marketing + WordPress eCommerce Website Redesign

Dutchess Bridle & Saddle is a well-established small business that was looking to increase their presence online through content marketing, and grow sales through eCommerce. I created comprehensive brand guidelines and implemented a thorough content marketing plan with social media postings, articles, and email campaigns. The original website had been created with GoDaddy's Web Builder, with a separate online store. In order to ensure a smooth experience for customers as well as ease of use for non-technical employees, I created a new website with integrated eCommerce store on WordPress.

Holstee Manifesto

HTML5 + CSS Code Snippet Exercise

The challenge: redesign the famous Holstee Manifesto using HTML and CSS. The original Holstee Manifesto is an iconic text design. For my interpretation, I used color, shape, and font to suggest a series of Post-It notes--the Manifesto's gentle reminders for mindful living made literal.
